# References and Bibliography

This section provides a comprehensive list of all external sources cited throughout the "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook." All citations adhere to **APA Style** guidelines.

---

## How to Cite from this Textbook

When citing information or code examples from this textbook, please use the following format:

**Book Citation (example):**

Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). *Title of textbook: Subtitle*. Publisher.

**Specific Chapter/Section Citation (example):**

Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter or section. In A. Editor, B. Editor, & C. Editor (Eds.), *Title of textbook: Subtitle* (pp. xxx-xxx). Publisher.
